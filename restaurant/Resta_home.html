Restaurant Description

Mama Kim's Burger is when Comfort food meets one of the best home chefs in the city of Louisville. Kim Jackson has been making grade A quality Grilled delights for her family for over 40 years, now she shares her delicious quisine with the world. There's no better place to eat Burgers, Brisket and other Grilled food favorites in Kentucky. Delicious sides that include famous cut-steak fries, Cole Slaw, and Salads that will delight. Kentucky is known for chicken but the best food comes from the grill. Welcome to our grill hut.
